Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers! Check out our FAQ section to find useful information about our real estate services and process.

1. How can I properly value my property before I put it up for sale?

To properly assess the value of your property before selling it, it is important to analyze similar properties in the area and consider the overall condition of the home. A professional appraisal from a certified appraiser can provide an objective estimate, and consulting with local real estate agents will give you valuable information about market trends.

2. What are the steps to buy a property through the agency?

Typically you will need proof of identity, proof of funds or a mortgage pre-approval, and any documents your lender requests. We walk you through the full checklist for your specific transaction.
