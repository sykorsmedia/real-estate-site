Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers! Check out our FAQ section to find useful information about our real estate services and process.

1. How can I properly value my property before I put it up for sale?

Start with a comparative market analysis: review recent sales of similar homes nearby, adjust for condition and upgrades, and consider current demand. We can provide a professional valuation and pricing strategy so you list with confidence.

2. What documents do I need when buying a property?

Typically you will need proof of identity, proof of funds or a mortgage pre-approval, and any documents your lender requests. We walk you through the full checklist for your specific transaction.

3. How long does it usually take to sell a home?

Timeline varies by location, price, and presentation. A well-priced, well-marketed home often attracts strong interest within the first few weeks. We set clear milestones so you always know what to expect.

4. Do you help with rentals as well as sales?

Yes. We support residential and commercial leases, from marketing and tenant screening to lease agreements and handover.

5. Can you assist with staging and photography?

We coordinate professional photography and can recommend staging options so your property makes a strong first impression online and in person.

6. How do I schedule a viewing?

Contact us by phone, email, or the form on this page. We will confirm a time that works for you and prepare everything needed for the visit.
